Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Vala e Kuqe application ("we", "our", "the application") handles information when it is used to publish our own video content to social platforms, including TikTok via the TikTok Content Posting API.

1. Information we access

When you connect a TikTok account to the application, we access:

  • Your TikTok authorization tokens (access token and refresh token) issued by TikTok after you grant permission;
  • Basic account information returned by TikTok, such as your display name and open ID, used to confirm the connected account;
  • The video files and accompanying captions that you choose to publish through the application.

We do not collect your TikTok password. Authorization is handled entirely by TikTok's official OAuth flow.

2. How we use this information

The information above is used solely to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok on your behalf;
  • Upload videos you have selected to your own TikTok account, either as drafts or as posts, using the TikTok Content Posting API;
  • Maintain your session by refreshing the access token when it expires.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to publishing your own content.

3. Storage and retention

Authorization tokens are stored locally in the environment where the application runs and are used only to communicate with TikTok. Tokens are retained only as long as needed to operate the application and are deleted when you disconnect the account or revoke access.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. Data is transmitted only between the application and TikTok's official APIs to perform the actions you request.

5. Revoking access

You can revoke the application's access at any time from your TikTok account settings, under "Security and login" → "Manage app permissions". Once revoked, the application can no longer access your account.

6. Children's privacy

The application is not directed to individuals under the age required by TikTok's own Terms of Service, and we do not knowingly process their data.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

8. Contact

For any question about this Privacy Policy, contact us at valaekuqe@gmail.com.
